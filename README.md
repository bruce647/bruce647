## About

Founding Engineer at **[Fortrip AI](https://fortrip.ai)**, building AI-powered travel agent platforms with multi-agent orchestration (Agno, AutoGen, Google A2A). Previously co-founded **EIO AI** — shipping multi-agent systems on AI display glasses running Android OS. Shipped products that hit **#1 on Product Hunt** and served **400+ enterprise clients** at **[Currents AI](https://currents.one)**.

I care about systems that work at scale: resumable AI streaming, modular component architecture, and CI/CD pipelines that keep teams moving fast.
